About Khan Academy on a Stick

What is Khan Academy on a Stick?

Using the website khanacademy.org requires a decent internet connection. Khan Academy on a Stick is made for situations where internet connection is poor or completely missing to help and support educational needs.

It is currently being used in Myanmar(Burma).

Who maintains Khan Academy on a Stick?

This offline version of Khan Academy was created by norberto@mujica.org. Ole Tange (ole@tange.dk) updated it to use the free video format WebM and made it fit on DVDs.

The content is a subset of the one available in khanacademy.org. This latest version includes very comprehensive Math and Science video playlists as they were available online on 2012.

There is no promised scheduled or planned maintenance, though all efforts will be made, time permitting, to keep its content updated.

What browsers can be used with this version?

Firefox is included (Windows) (Mac), but any modern browser that support the free WebM video format can be used. A full list can be found on en.wikipedia.org/wiki/HTML5_video.

How can I send my feedback or comments about Khan Academy on a Stick?

Please send any comments or suggestions to ole@tange.dk and norberto@mujica.org. Your feedback will be greatly appreciated.
